How It Works

Donate your clothes
in three simple steps.

No trips to the charity shop. No trips to recycling centre. Just book, bag, and we'll do the rest — completely free.

STEP 01

Book Your Free Collection

Fill in our quick online form and pick a date that suits you. We'll confirm your slot within one working day. Takes under 2 minutes — no phone calls needed.

STEP 02

Bag It & Leave It Out

On your chosen day, bag up your unwanted clothes and leave them on your doorstep before we arrive. No sorting, no labels — any condition, any style accepted.

STEP 03

We Sort, Recycle & Give Back

Our team collects your bags, sorts every item responsibly, and ensures nothing goes to landfill. Clothes are reused, recycled, or redirected to support charitable causes across Wales.

About Us

Built for South Wales

Subnex Group is a South Wales-based company specialising in textile collection clothing bank placement and regular, reliable collections across the region. We work with households, businesses, and community organisations to make clothes donation genuinely easy.

Part of what we do supports charitable causes across Wales — so every bag you fill does more good than you might think.
